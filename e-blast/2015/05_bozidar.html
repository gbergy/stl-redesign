SHOOT THE LOBSTER

UNISEX BASICS BY BOZIDAR BRAZDA

Opening Reception: Thursday, February 12, 6-8pm

Collection also available online as of March
Shoot The Lobster Store

From February 12th to March 1st, artist Bozidar Brazda presents a collection of unisex basics available in — cult-member white, bondage black and beet-juice pink — at the Shoot The Lobster storefront on Eldridge.

Bozidar Brazda has exhibited at MoMA, The Whitney Museum of American Art, The Kitchen and other venues in New York and abroad. His work has been featured in Interview, Purple, Vogue, The New York Times, Architectural Digest and Maximum Rock 'N' Roll.
