for International Loner



international loner is playing the jingle
of free tub porn
on the piano with us
with a cool gaze out at the lazy city traffic
hands tired
from looking for some thing black



the tilt of the hips
your hat just so
some thing black



little loner
every idea you ever had fits
exactly into any black hole on the canvas



if you’re a girl



international loner
is a thousand different animals
all ready to carry
your soulstuff



If you find yourself surrounded
the cats paw is an effective tool
for clawing your way out
or shifting the fabric against your groin
folding the ends away
to reveal some thing black



show without end
softness around the bend



when the TV sets are lifted from the ground
the creaking they make
I swear
is the sound of sweet
perfumed spring rain outside your secret day job



fresh soft winds of inspiration
in each new city
and holes are a part of this world



excellent gentleman from the provinces
when you
turn into an animal
there will be no more wilderness



I believe when I gaze innovatively into space as I am doing at this moment, it is all that truly matters.



I crept into the garden here exhausted
And when the night enfolded me so sweetly—



Lovely, isn’t it?



Text: Sebastian Rozenberg
